We are mySociety

Hi! That’s us. We’re Liz, and Zarino, and Gareth, and Mark (there are two Marks actually, and two Daves) and Gemma, and Louise, and—

Well let’s put it this way. We’re a bunch of people building online technologies and sharing them in places where they can make a difference.

Or, in the well-crafted words of our mission statement:

We build and popularise digital tools that empower people worldwide.

As you can see, we’re a small team. But for the last twelve months, every single one of us has been beavering away like… well… beavers. And we’ve achieved a lot.
